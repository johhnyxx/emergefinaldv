Anger Management

Emerge started offering Anger Management services in 2002. The anger management program is designed for:

  • People who have been violent in a barroom brawl or other        fights with strangers
  • People who have resisted arrest and gotten into fights with      police officers, when the arrest charge did not
         involve domestic violence
  • People who have road rage issues
  • People who have low-grade anger toward everyone, but are      not abusive in their relationships
  • People who have been required to complete an anger      management group by the court or child protective services      (DCF)

    • Enroll Now!

    To enroll in the Anger Management program at Emerge, call our office at (617) 547-9879 or email us at info@emergedv.com, We will conduct a brief screening by asking your reasons for coming to Emerge, and then we will set up a time for the program orientation.

    Anger Management orientations are by appointment only, and participants must pay the $25 orientation fee to their orientation appointment. Please use the PayPal buttons on the main page to make this payment. Orientation appointments are approximately 60 minutes in duration and are conducted via Zoom.
